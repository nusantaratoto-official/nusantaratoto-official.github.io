Understanding Both Teams to Score on Nusantaratoto

Both Teams to Score, often called BTTS, is a binary market: you predict whether both teams will score at least one goal each during the match. If the final score is 2–1, 3–2, or 1–1, both teams scored and your bet wins. If the result is 1–0 or 0–0, your bet loses. On Nusantaratoto, we display BTTS odds alongside traditional match outcomes, so you can compare markets in seconds.

The appeal lies in simplicity. You don't need to predict the winner or the exact scoreline — just whether both teams will find the net. This makes BTTS popular during high-scoring tournaments like Liga 1 and Piala AFF, where attacking play is common. On our mobile platform, we update odds in real time as the match progresses, so you can place live bets if you're watching on your phone.

Both Teams to Score Rules and Settlement

On Nusantaratoto, Both Teams to Score bets settle based on the final score at the end of regular time. Extra time and penalty shootouts do not count — only the 90-minute result matters. If a match is abandoned or postponed, your bet is typically voided and your stake returned, though we recommend checking the specific fixture's terms on our platform.

Own goals count toward the team that benefits from them. For example, if Team A scores 2 goals and Team B scores 1 own goal (credited to Team B), both teams have scored and BTTS bets win. Red cards and injuries don't affect settlement — only the final scoreline matters.
